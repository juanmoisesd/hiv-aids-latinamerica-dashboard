### New Open Dataset Published: Interactive dashboard: HIV/AIDS epidemiological data in Latin America (2000-2020). DOI: 10.7910/DVN/99WRWQ

**Universidad Internacional de La Rioja (UNIR)** — Juan Moisés de la Serna Tuya (ORCID: https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8401-8018) has published a new open dataset: *Interactive dashboard: HIV/AIDS epidemiological data in Latin America (2000-2020). DOI: 10.7910/DVN/99WRWQ*.
